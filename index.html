Tamper protection — not yet configured
Tamper protection locks Defender settings so that malware, ransomware, and even local admins cannot disable real-time protection, cloud protection, or behavior monitoring. Without it, sophisticated malware kills Defender before doing damage. This is a 2-minute change.
+2 pts free
